Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 2026

All paid consulting sessions are non-refundable. Sessions may be rescheduled once, subject to the conditions below.

1. No-Refund Policy

All payments made for consulting sessions — whether the 30-minute (₹999) or 60-minute (₹1,999) session — are final and non-refundable once the payment is processed.

This applies regardless of whether the client:

  • Changes their mind after payment
  • Fails to attend the scheduled session
  • Does not book a slot within the validity period
  • Cancels with less than 24 hours' notice

2. Rescheduling Policy

Each paid session may be rescheduled once, provided the following conditions are met:

  • The reschedule request is made at least 24 hours before the originally scheduled session time
  • The new session is booked within 30 days of the original booking date
  • The request is sent via email to aakankshakarande@yahoo.com

Rescheduling requests made with less than 24 hours' notice will not be accommodated and the session will be considered completed.

3. No-Show Policy

If you do not attend your scheduled session without prior notice, the session is forfeited. No reschedule or refund will be offered in this case.

4. Cancellation by Aakanksha Karande

In the rare event that I need to cancel a confirmed session, I will:

  • Notify you at least 12 hours in advance wherever possible
  • Offer a full reschedule at a time convenient to you, or
  • Issue a complete refund if no suitable reschedule time can be agreed upon

This is the only scenario in which a refund will be issued.

5. Session Validity

Once payment is made, the session credit is valid for 30 days. If no slot is booked within this period, the session is forfeited and no refund or extension will be provided.

6. How to Request a Reschedule

To reschedule your session, email aakankshakarande@yahoo.com with:

  • Your full name
  • Original session date and time
  • Payment reference number (from Razorpay)
  • Your preferred new date and time

I will confirm the reschedule within 24 hours on business days.

7. Disputes

If you believe a charge was made in error, please contact me at aakankshakarande@yahoo.com within 7 days of the transaction. I will review and respond within 3 business days.

For payment disputes initiated through Razorpay or your bank, please note that this no-refund policy will be referenced in all such cases.

8. Contact

For any questions about this policy, write to aakankshakarande@yahoo.com. I aim to respond within 1–2 business days.
